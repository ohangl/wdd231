Site Plan for Personal Portfolio

1. Site Name

angela's portfolio

Chosen for clear personal branding and technical focus. Simple and direct. (Still dont know, maybe it would be OHANGL'S portfolio)

2. Site Purpose

To show my web development skills and real projects, providing employers or clients with an easy way to review my work and contact me.

3. Scenarios

  • Hiring Manager: “Which projects demonstrate your React and Node.js experience?”
  • Client: “How can I reach you to discuss a freelance web project?”
  • Potential Client: “I want to see your projects to know if them match for what I am looking for.”

4. Color Schema

  • Primary – Black (#000000): Headings, main text.
  • Secondary – White (#FFFFFF): Page background.
  • Accent – Gray (#808080): Borders, icons, hover states.
  • Muted – Light Gray (#F4F4F4): Section backgrounds.

5. Typography

Montserrat (Headings)

Applied to all <h1>–<h3> for a clean, modern feel.

Open Sans (Body)

Used for paragraphs, lists, and form text to ensure readability.
